what does Sales Solutions Marketing team do


Sales Solutions Marketing team (often synonymous with Solutions Marketing) bridges the gap between traditional product marketing and the sales department. Unlike teams that focus solely on individual product features, this team focuses on the buyer’s vision for success by bundling products, services, and expertise into comprehensive answers for complex business problems. 


Core Responsibilities
The team typically operates across three primary pillars:
* Solution Positioning & Messaging: They translate technical product features into high-level value stories that resonate with executive decision-makers. They position the customer as the "hero" and the company’s solution as the "guide" to overcoming strategic challenges.
* Sales Enablement: They equip sales teams with a consultative narrative rather than just product knowledge. This includes creating specialized materials like:
   * ROI calculators to prove financial value.
   * Case studies and "solution perspective" documents that illustrate real-world problem-solving.
   * Objection-handling resources tailored to specific industries or business dynamics.
* Strategic Demand Generation: Instead of generic messaging, they develop targeted campaigns that address high-level business needs and industry-specific "pain points".
